About This Project

The Mission

Visual Effects Showcase is dedicated to exploring the intersection of modern design and web performance. We believe that beautiful interfaces shouldn't compromise on speed or accessibility.

Our goal is to provide high-quality tutorials and code examples for developers looking to push the boundaries of what's possible with vanilla CSS and JavaScript.

Who We Are

We are a small team of frontend enthusiasts who love experimenting with the latest browser APIs and CSS specifications. From Glassmorphism to complex 3D transforms, we break down advanced concepts into digestible guides.
